When to Seek Professional Help

These practices support emotional well-being but are not therapy. Consider seeking professional help if you're experiencing:

  • Persistent sadness, anxiety, or emotional pain that won't ease
  • Difficulty functioning in daily life (work, relationships, self-care)
  • Trauma that feels too big to process alone
  • Thoughts of self-harm or harming others
  • Substance use as a coping mechanism
  • Symptoms of mental health conditions (depression, anxiety, PTSD, etc.)
  • Patterns that repeat despite your best efforts at self-work
