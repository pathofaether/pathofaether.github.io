Name What Drains You

Take your paper and pen. Write at the top: "What is not mine to carry."

Beneath it, list what you've been holding that doesn't belong to you:

  • Other people's emotions or problems
  • Obligations you said yes to out of guilt
  • Relationships that deplete rather than nourish
  • Expectations that aren't aligned with your truth

Be honest. No one will see this but you. Name what drains you without softening it.

"I release what is not mine to hold."

Visualize Your Shield

"My energy is sacred. I choose what I allow in."

Practice Your No

Say it aloud: "No."

Again, louder: "No."

One more time with your full voice: "No."

Feel how it lands in your body. Notice if guilt rises‚Äîit will. Breathe through it. Your no is not cruel. It is clarity.

Now practice a complete boundary:

"I can't take that on right now."
"That doesn't work for me."
"I need space to consider that."

You don't owe anyone an explanation. Your no is enough.

"No is a complete sentence. I honor my limits."
