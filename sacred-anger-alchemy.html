Burn or Transform

If you wrote something, you have two options:

Burn it safely: If using a real candle, hold the paper over the flame or use a fire-safe dish. If using alternatives, use a lighter or matches in a safe outdoor space. Watch the words turn to ash. As they dissolve, know that the energy has been released.

Or tear it up: Rip the paper into pieces. Throw them away or bury them in the earth. The act of destruction is the transformation.

As the ashes fall or paper tears, say aloud:
